标题: Resolution of coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19).

作者: Khaled Habas, Chioma Nganwuchu, Fanila Shahzad, Rajendran Gopalan, Mainul Haque, Sayeeda Rahman, Anwarul Azim Majumder, Talat Nasim

摘要: Coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) was first detected in China in December, 2019, and declared as a pandemic by the World Health Organization (WHO) on March 11, 2020. The current management of COVID-19 is based generally on supportive therapy and treatment to prevent respiratory failure. The effective option of antiviral therapy and vaccination are currently under evaluation and development.
