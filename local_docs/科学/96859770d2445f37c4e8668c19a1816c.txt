标题: Current Conversion Technologies and Case Studies

作者: Chávez-González, Mónica L., Aguilar-Zarate, Pedro, Haghi, A. K.

发表日期: 2026-01-01

摘要: Agro-food waste is a valuable resource with significant energy recovery potential. The integration of advanced technologies and real-world applications, as illustrated in these case studies, is paving the way for a sustainable energy future. These methods not only reduce waste but also help meet energy demands, supporting both environmental and economic goals. If you want to dive deeper, the references provided in each section can offer detailed insights into these innovative practices.
