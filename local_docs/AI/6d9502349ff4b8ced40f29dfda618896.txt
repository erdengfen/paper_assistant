标题: Explicit construction of recurrent neural networks effectively
  approximating discrete dynamical systems

作者: Chikara Nakayama, Tsuyoshi Yoneda

发表日期: 2024-09-28

摘要: We consider arbitrary bounded discrete time series originating from dynamical
system with recursivity. More precisely, we provide an explicit construction of
recurrent neural networks which effectively approximate the corresponding
discrete dynamical systems.
